About Me



I’m Jocelyn, I use she/her pronouns and I'm a mechanical engineering graduate from the University of Alberta. In 2020, I created my YouTube channel to share my experiences as a queer person of colour in engineering. I also created a separate YouTube channel to post my music covers. This eventually led me to explore other creative hobbies such as film photography and web development.

I have many hobbies including dancing, skateboarding, and snowboarding and I also love to learn new skills. I am especially intrigued by anything that involves design, so I decided to code this website to showcase my artistic endeavours.

Take a look around! I hope you enjoy!

Music

I began playing the violin and piano at a young age and I started playing the guitar and drums in high school/university. Check out my music channel on YouTube to listen to my covers.

Photography

I began learning learning about film photography in 2020 and it has since become my favourite medium to shoot with. Check out my photography instagram for some of my favourite photos.

Web Development

I started learning about web development in June 2024 through FreeCodeCamp and Udemy. I'm still VERY new to this, but I love exploring how websites are created. This is my first website and I'm proud of what I've created so far!

Dance

I started lindy hop (swing dancing) in 2018 and I have also taken hip hop, waacking, and house classes. I love expressing myself through movement and I hope to continue dancing for as long as possible!

Skateboarding & Snowboarding

I love being on a board and I am grateful for the comunity that I have found within these sports. Be sure to check out the Tigers Skate Club, which is a group that fosters the growth of women and 2SLGBTQ+ folks in skateboarding.
